## Straight flows — research & fix checklist

Observed in `output/pizza-order-process.bpmn` (generated by the `executable` prompt):
after `layout_bpmn_diagram` the `orthogonalFlowPercent` was only **63 %** and the
exported XML contains Z-shaped and diagonal segments between same-row elements.

### Root cause

`moveElementTo` calls `modeling.moveElements` which applies bpmn-js's **10 px grid
snap on the element top-left**.  Because different element types have different
heights, the snapped top-left lands at different distances from the intended
center Y:

| Type     | height | target center Y | top-left before snap | snapped top-left | actual center Y | drift |
|----------|--------|-----------------|----------------------|------------------|-----------------|-------|
| Task     | 80     | 200             | 160                  | 160 ✓            | 200             | 0 px  |
| Gateway  | 50     | 200             | 175                  | 180              | **205**         | +5 px |
| Event    | 36     | 200             | 182                  | 180              | **198**         | −2 px |

The 2–5 px center drift causes `modeling.layoutConnection` (ManhattanLayout) to
produce Z-shaped waypoints instead of straight horizontal lines.
